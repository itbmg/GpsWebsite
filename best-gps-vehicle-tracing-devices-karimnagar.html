Transportation is the core of many businesses from manufacturing to FMCG to reach their products to the last mile.

GoTracking, as the name suggests, GPS/GPRS vehicle tracking solution, started to address the problems facing by transportation industry. GPS positioning with lot of other inputs like ignition, AC, temperature can be monitored in real-time from vehicle.


How does GPS/GPRS vehicle tracking work?

Essentially, hardware contains a GPS chip which acquires latitude and longitude or Geo coordinates from GPS satellites. Along with the Geo coordinates other data like ignition, AC, temperature, fuel level of the vehicle is transmitted to telemetrics cloud servers using GSM Network This data will be analysed and presented in an intuitive dashboard and report to the stakeholders in the system.
