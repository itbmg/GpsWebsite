Best GPS Tracking Solutions for Cabs/Taxi Dispatch

How GoTracking is Helping in Safety for Cab passengers:
Cabs have become the most important mode of transportation in almost all the cities and towns, Fleet owners and Cab operating Companies operate services 24/7 as these services became necessary for present Generation. To fulfill this necessity, GPS Cab Tracking Solution came into a picture, as punctuality, fast boarding, Electronic Mileage Calculation, Route track and SOS alerts are the primary necessities of the end customer, Cabs enabled with GPS Vehicle Tracking Solution serves all the needs.



Salient Features of Cab Tracking Solution:

  1. Ease of handling operations to fleet owners.
  2. Shift wise/ Driver Wise Reports.
  3. Increased performance or Efficiency of Cabs/drivers
  4. Trip wise reports
  5. Driver wise trip reports
  6. Vehicle/driver login and logout timings
  7. Real Time Updates
  8. Geo-Fence Alerts
